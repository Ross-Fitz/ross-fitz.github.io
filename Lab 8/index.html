Welcome to the game of ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

The rules for Rock Paper Scissors are as follows:

  • If you (the player) picks rock, and the oppenent picks paper, paper beats rock so as a reult, you lose. However, if your oppenent picks scissors and picked rock then rock beats scissors and you will win.
  • If you pick paper and the other person picks scissors, you loose. If you pick paper and the opponent picks rock, you win.
  • If you pick scissors and your opponent picks rock, you lose. If you pick scissors and the other player chooses paper, you win.
  • If you both pick the same one (rock+rock, paper+paper, scissors+scissors) then it is a draw and you will have to play again.
